1. How many people attended and what was the split between in-person and virtual?

Pie chart shows the split between virtual attendees and in-person attendees

• A total of 2786 Delegates attended the event, 2382 in person and 404 Delegates attended online sessions, there is possibility of people attending in person but also viewing online.

2. Average watch time online

The bar chart shows the Virtual Average Watch Time for every session run in the event

• Plenary Session was by far the most popular session in terms of total delegates and average watch time, but it was also the longest of all the sessions, so this explains why it has the highest average watch time. (So was not included in the bar chart)

3. Average watch time online for sessions that ran for 20 minutes

The bar chart is used to show the Virtual Average Watch Time for every session that had a duration of 20 minutes

• The overall average watch time for sessions was quite low compared to the duration of each session. When filtered for the sessions which ran for 20 minutes, it was seen that the highest watch time was 15.9 min for the Workday session and the lowest was 2.6 min for the BT session.

• This shows that many people were not watching the whole session, the average of the watch time per delegate was 9.5 minutes. This means that on average the delegates only watched 48% of the session.

4. Total delegates in each online session

The bar chart shows the Total Virtual Attendees for each session that took place

• Plenary session had the highest number of delegates who viewed the session at least once, but this is probably due to being the longest session.

• Oracle had the second highest with 256 people viewing but only had an average watch time of 9.8 minutes for the 35-minute-long session so many of the delegates did not stay for the majority of this session
